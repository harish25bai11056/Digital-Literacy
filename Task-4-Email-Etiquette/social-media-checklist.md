# Social Media Best Practices for College Students

This checklist provides a framework for maintaining a professional and responsible digital presence during your academic career.

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Audit your privacy settings** regularly to control who sees your personal posts. | **Don't post illegal content**, such as underage drinking or illicit substances. |
| **Network with professionals** and alumni in your field via platforms like LinkedIn. | **Don't engage in "flame wars"** or public arguments that could be perceived as bullying. |
| **Highlight your achievements**, such as Dean's List honors, projects, or volunteer work. | **Don't vent about professors or employers**—the internet is permanent and searchable. |
| **Use a professional profile picture** (a clear headshot) for networking platforms. | **Don't share sensitive personal info** like your specific dorm number or daily schedule. |
| **Think before you share**—verify information to ensure you aren't spreading misinformation. | **Don't use offensive language** or post content that violates campus codes of conduct. |
